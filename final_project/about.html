About

"Hazbin Hotel," created by Vivienne "VivziePop" Medrano, is an animated web series that invites viewers into a fantastical and chaotic rendition of Hell. The story centers around Charlie, the princess of Hell, and her ambitious venture to rehabilitate demons through the Hazbin Hotel. The pilot episode, "That's Entertainment," introduces viewers to the lively and eccentric characters populating this underworld, offering a unique blend of dark humor, vibrant animation, and catchy musical numbers. The series explores themes of redemption, self-discovery, and the complexities of demon existence. With its distinctive visual style, engaging characters, and a narrative that skillfully balances comedic elements with underlying depth, Hazbin Hotel has captivated audiences, making it a standout creation in the realm of online animation.

Purpose

The overall puprose of the Hazbin hotel in the show, was to rehabilitate sinners. Charlie Morningstar's goal is taken up by this hotel. In the pilot episode, the only resident she and her girlfriend, Vaggie, have, is a sinner named Angel Dust. Vaggie shares these goals with Charlie, and serves as her backup through evey plan Charlie has, including attempting to talk to heaven, which backfires horribly in episode 1.
