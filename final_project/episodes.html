Episodes 1-4

In the first four episodes, we're introduced to our cast, retold the story of how heaven and hell began, and the goal of the story. In the first episode, Charlie attempts to convince heaven to help with her plan to rehabilitate sinners, and she is rudely shut down by the angel, Adam. In the second episode, villians send Sir Pentious, the snake demon, undercover into the hotel to find out what Alastor is doing. He fails however, and is caught, but is welcomed into the hotel to be rehabilitated. In our third episode, Alastor is called to an overlords meeting, where it is found out that Angels can be killed, so now theere is a way to fight back against the yearly extermination that heaven sends down. In the fourth episode, Angel struggles to juggle his work and his time at the Hotel. Charlie decides it's time to put her 'Princess' status to use with Angel's boss, Valentino, another overlord.

Episodes 5-8

In the fifth episode, Charlie reunites with her father, Lucifer, and he gets her a meeting with heaven. In the sixth, we're introduced to heaven, and two of the Seraphim, Sera and Emily. In this episode, there is a court ruling and it is ruled against Charlie. Coming up to the seventh episode, Charlie and Vaggie attempt to raise an army to protect themselves against the Angels coming in the extermination. Finally, in our eigth and final episode of the season, there is a battle between Heaven and hell, and Charlie's goal is fulfilled, in that a sinner is sent to heaven.
