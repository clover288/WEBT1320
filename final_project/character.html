Characters

Charlie Morningstar

Charlotte "Charlie" Morningstar is the main character in the musical animated series Hazbin Hotel. She is the princess of Hell, the founder of the Happy Hotel, and the daughter of Lucifer Morningstar and Lilith. Charlie's powers include demon transformation and she tries to reform sinners. Her goal is to redeem the patrons of her hotel so they can go to Heaven. Alastor helps Charlie rehabilitate monsters, but their motivations differ. Charlie seeks redemption for the underworld's inhabitants, whereas Alastor primarily pursues his own interests. Charlie's powers are not as strong as some of the other characters in the show, such as Alastor or Angel Dust. However, she is still a valuable asset to the team.

Angel Dust

Angel Dust is a charming and flamboyant Sinner known for his sassy personality and eye-catching fashion sense. Despite his seemingly carefree demeanor, Angel Dust harbors a complex past and struggles with aspects of his demon existence. His presence in the Hazbin Hotel, a rehabilitation center for wayward souls, adds depth to the narrative as he navigates the challenges of redemption in the chaotic underworld of Hell. With his quick wit, sharp tongue, and unexpected vulnerability, Angel Dust contributes to the series' blend of dark humor, vibrant character dynamics, and the overarching theme of personal growth and redemption.

Alastor

Alastor Hartfelt, also known as "The Radio Demon", is a character in the animated web series Hazbin Hotel. He is a sinner demon and one of the most notorious Overlords of Hell. Alastor is known for his wide, toothy grin, beige skin, and sharp yellow teeth. Alastor's moral alignment is complex and open to interpretation. He is often portrayed as an antagonist or anti-hero rather than a traditional hero or villain. Alastor is a mysterious dealmaker who offers to assist Charlie Morningstar in her endeavors for his own amusement. Alastor's hotel position is "Facilities Manager", despite Charlie being the only other member of the staff who is stronger than him.
