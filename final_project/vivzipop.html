YOUTUBE ACCOUNT

VivziePop's YouTube account is a vibrant and imaginative platform that showcases the artistic talents of its creator, Vivienne "VivziePop" Medrano. Renowned for her distinctive animation style and storytelling prowess, VivziePop has gained a significant following on YouTube. The channel is home to a variety of animated projects, including the widely acclaimed "Hazbin Hotel" and "Helluva Boss." These series explore unique and fantastical worlds populated by colorful characters, often delving into dark humor and themes. VivziePop's animations are characterized by fluid motion, detailed character designs, and a penchant for pushing creative boundaries. The channel not only serves as a showcase for her creative endeavors but also provides a platform for fans to engage with and appreciate the artistic vision of VivziePop. With a dedicated fanbase, the YouTube account continues to be a hub for animation enthusiasts and those captivated by VivziePop's imaginative storytelling.

Her first animations posted where renditions of Keshas original song "Die young" and "Timber." Both beautifully done and well choreographed, they feature bright colors, a large array of characters, and very different scenes then you would expect. These caught traction online, primarily on youtube, and she became big off of those and the hazbin hotel pilot.

Vivzie now does fan panels at conventions where she leaves the board up for questions. Often she is asked about upcoming projects of hers, and details about videos and lore from previous projects. She often reveals lore and secrets at these conventions, so you can get a jist of whats about to happen. She has a large fanbase and following on nearly every platform. She has begun working with amazon in making hazbin hotel, and the next season will be released soon.
